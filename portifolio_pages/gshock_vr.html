Gshock Xperience

Made with unity and C#

Gshock xperience is an advergame made for Cassio’s about their new Gshock model. I worked as a Unity 3D generalist and C# programmer!

Gshock Xperience is a game developed on Unity 3D where the player should rescue the new Cassio’s wristwatch model from dangerous situations. The user is situated in a futurist factory where the challenges will appear. The project was a competition that lasted 15 days in a São Paulo’s mall where, at the end, the player with the highest score in the game won a real Gshock.

The challenges consisted in 3 stages: Set the new model from an giant truck that was above him making pression; Take it away from a vat 200m height, where the wristwatch is submerged; And last, the player would have to walk on a straight platform at a enormous height to avoid the wristwatch from falling.
