Digital Architectural Models

Made with Unity, Unreal and C#, C++ and Blueprint

Digital Architecture Models are interactive models of real buildings in construction. The application shows how the building will look like in the future. I worked at all of these products as a Unity 3D/Unreal generalist, C#/C++/Blueprint programmer!

The Digital Architecture Models were developed on Unreal Engine and Unity 3D. I started making them on Unity but moved to Unreal after a while. The project is always made in conjunction with the construction company owner of the corresponding building. Using precise architecture mesures like real size and proportions, everything is made to be exactly like the one that will be constructed among other minor details.

My biggest work in this project was to create a navigation method that was practical and simple to anyone, that way, any kind of person, even someone with less technology knowledge, could use and understand the application.

All media shown here are from projects made on Unity and Unreal, but they all have a pretty similar operation.
