Touchscreen Application

Made with Unity and C#

Touchscreen Applications are interactive apps made for tablets or PCs that usually help with property sales. The app works like a guide to the customer, showing the owner company, the features of the realty and any other relevant information. I worked at this project as a C# programmer and Unity 3D generalist.

I developed this project several times, to many different clients. This app is often used on sales points that have little room space or just for easy transport and better demonstration of the clientâ€™s product. To facilitate the process, I developed the project in a way that I could change all the content in a quick and easy way, keeping the layout, organization and flow. So, besides making it easy to develop new versions, I could make it to new clients on a short deadline and constant quality.

The biggest challenge in developing this app was making it elegant and clean, and at the same time easy to use, so it can be used by any employee or final client itself.

All media shown here are from one project made on Unity 3D, but I developed many versions of this, just changing the content.
