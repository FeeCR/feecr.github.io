Construct VR

Made with Unity and C#

Construct VR is a gamified experience made for Direcional Construtora to promote their quality on buildings. In this project I worked as a Unity 3D generalist and C# programmer.

At this project made on Unity 3D the player is situated at a building under construction from a generic company. He must check the construction status and find out the problems, this causes the player to face several difficulties until he reaches an already done, and much more safe, building of Direcional Construtora. This project traveled to many states of Brazil with awards for the fastest players.

The player’s challenges involved climbing and a lot of height, provoking the feeling of fear and trying to delay the player! I used many sound effects like wind sounds, heartbeats and heavy breathing to help with the immersion of these feelings and cause the constant adrenaline sensation in the player.

For the award controlling, I developed a system that saved all new entries of players using their ID as a key on a worksheet. That way, if anyone tried to play again and get a better score (Earning a better reward), the program wouldn’t save his score, keeping the first one. Making sure that was fair, nobody takes more then 1 award and controlling the number of people that played.
