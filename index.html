About me

I have been working with Game Development for over 7 years, developing on Game Engines maily. I started making games as an artist on college projects, that led me to an experience on 3D modeling using Blender. Despite this, I kept studying programming and getting better at it!

Currently I’m working mainly as a generalist and programmer (C#) on Unity 3D developing Virtual Reality applications on gamified experiences and for corporate training. Also I make games for mobile/tablets and interactive applications on touchscreens. Regardless of my Unity 3D experience, I also work as a generalist and programmer (Blueprint and C ++) on Unreal Engine mainly on developing digital architectural models presentations for real estate agencies and some Virtual Reality games.

I’m really passionate about developing personal projects where I reproduce or create new mechanics and tools of games to speed up the development of a project. Always searching for the best and creative way to solve the problems that I encounter on my way!
